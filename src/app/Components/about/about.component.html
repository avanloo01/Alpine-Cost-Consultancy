Mission

To reduce societal costs to the bare minimum

Many consultancy projects end in an "investment proposal." Unfortunately, these reports are often the product of mindless babbling. At Alpine CCC, we focus on real components of the income statement: costs. Every balance consists of income and costs. We help businesses reduce their costs because lower costs provide more freedom of movement for a firm's core activities.

Strategy

To ethically serve 7 hospitality businesses in the first year of operations by eliminating unnecessary costs, based on the dedication of a workhorse and student frugality
